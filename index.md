Complex numbers are are numbers with a *real components* and an *imaginary component* a + bi where a and b are **real numbers** and i is the imaginary unit
An extended explanation of complex numbers can be found [here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complex_number).

It can be helpful to visualize complex numbers on the complex plane:
![complex_image](https://www.google.com/imgres?q=complex%20plane&imgurl=https%3A%2F%2Fupload.wikimedia.org%2Fwikipedia%2Fcommons%2F5%2F5d%2FImaginarynumber2.PNG&imgrefurl=https%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fwiki%2FComplex_plane&docid=eVKFJpuliGjuuM&tbnid=nhXs9MHIz3l9UM&vet=12ahUKEwj8vu_LuNeVAxVm1vACHWNAHMQQnPAOegQIOhAA..i&w=265&h=220&hcb=2&ved=2ahUKEwj8vu_LuNeVAxVm1vACHWNAHMQQnPAOegQIOhAA)
