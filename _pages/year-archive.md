This project connects to my interests in physics, astronomy, and computational research. I want to understand how quantum algorithms and optimization can be applied to real scientific challenges.

I hope to build a stronger foundation in quantum information science and explore how emerging technologies may influence future research. Studying the possible role of quantum computing in exoplanet exploration would allow me to combine my interest in space with computation.

In the future, I hope to continue studying physics and computational science, particularly areas involving theoretical modeling, astronomy, and advanced mathematical techniques. This project helps me explore how new computational methods can contribute to groundbreaking discoveries about our universe.
